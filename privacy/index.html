Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 1, 2025

Overview

Moose Browser (“we”, “us”, “our”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

Email address: If you join our beta waitlist or subscribe to product updates, we collect your email address. We use it solely to send you your beta invite and product updates.

Usage analytics: We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage data to improve the browser. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your browsing history, search queries, passwords, or any personal content you view or enter in the browser.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses Google Tag Manager for analytics. These services may set cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

Data Storage

Email addresses submitted through our website are stored securely and are not shared with or sold to third parties.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing info@moosebrowser.com.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at info@moosebrowser.com.
