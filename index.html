FAQ

Questions, answered.

A quick overview of pricing, privacy, and platform support before you try Moose.

Is Moose free?

Moose is free to use, but some features are limited. For the full experience and to support development, please consider buying a license.

How do I activate my license?

Buy a license first on the purchase page. Then open Moose Browser, click the Settings icon, choose Activate License, and enter your license key.

Does it track anything?

Currently, Moose does not track anything. We may introduce anonymized telemetry in the future with an option to opt out.

Which OS is supported?

Currently macOS. Windows and Linux are coming soon.

Where do I report a bug or problem?

Send us an email at info@moosebrowser.com.
